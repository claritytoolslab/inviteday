Your RSVP data is protected with bank-level security:

  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: All RSVP responses are encrypted on your device before transmission
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Only event creators can decrypt RSVP data. We cannot read attendee information
  • Client-Side Keys: Encryption keys are generated on your device and never leave it
  • Privacy First: Invited guests cannot see other people's RSVP responses
